Electic Bycycle

To address the ₹30,000 Cr Indian bicycle market dominated by unbranded products with premium, connected EV cycles under Acer's name.

Model Summary:

  • Type: Distributor Investment Model
  • Investment: ₹30 Lakhs
  • Deployed Across: 10 dealers (₹3L inventory per dealer, 10-12 cycles each)

Returns

  • Guaranteed: 2% per month (₹60,000)
  • Or 30% of monthly net profit, whichever is higher

Why It Works

  • • No upfront cost for dealers → faster inventory movement
  • • Investors remain passive; Acer handles distribution, tracking, and support
  • • Cycles include Shimano brakes, Yamaha motors, Panasonic batteries

Target: Thousands of high-street dealers across India

Value Prop: First brand offering 3-year anti-rust warranty in cycles
